## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I connect my gaming platforms?**

A: Go to Settings â†’ Platform Connections and tap on the platform you want to connect:
- **PlayStation**: Sign in with your PSN account
- **Xbox**: Sign in with your Xbox Live account  
- **Steam**: Enter your Steam ID or custom URL

**Q: Is my login information secure?**

A: Yes! We use OAuth authentication through official platform APIs. We never store your passwords. All data is encrypted and transmitted securely.

**Q: Why can't I see all my games?**

A: Make sure your profile privacy settings allow public viewing:
- **PSN**: Set games/trophies to "Anyone" in Privacy Settings
- **Xbox**: Set gamertag to "Everyone" in Xbox Privacy & Online Safety
- **Steam**: Set profile to "Public" in Steam Privacy Settings

### Syncing & Updates

**Q: How often can I sync my data?**

A:
- **Free Users**: PSN (3x/day), Xbox/Steam (every 1 hour)
- **Premium Users**: All platforms (every 15 minutes)

**Q: My sync is stuck or failing. What should I do?**

A: Try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify platform privacy settings
3. Force close and restart the app
4. Sign out and sign back in to the affected platform
5. Wait a few minutes and try again (rate limits may apply)

**Q: Some of my achievements/trophies are missing.**

A: This can happen if:
- The game was recently played (sync every few hours)
- Your profile is private on that platform
- The platform API is experiencing delays
- DLC content hasn't been synced yet

Try syncing again after ensuring your profile is public.

### Features

**Q: What is StatusXP Score?**

A: StatusXP Score is a unified metric that combines your achievements across all connected platforms into one prestigious number. It factors in:
- Total trophies/achievements
- Platinum trophies
- Ultra-rare unlocks
- Completion rates
- Game variety

**Q: What is the Flex Room?**

A: The Flex Room is your personal gaming museum where you curate and showcase your most impressive achievements. Choose from categories like:
- Rarest Unlock
- Longest Grind
- Proudest Platinum
- Hardest Boss
- First Love

The app provides smart suggestions based on your actual achievements.

**Q: How do AI Achievement Guides work?**

A: When viewing any game's achievements, tap the AI Guide button to get personalized step-by-step strategies. The AI analyzes the achievement requirements and provides:
- Detailed walkthroughs
- Tips and strategies
- Common pitfalls to avoid
- Estimated time/difficulty

Free users get 3 AI guides per day. Premium users get unlimited access.

**Q: What are Meta-Achievements?**

A: Meta-Achievements are StatusXP-exclusive achievements you unlock by using the app:
- Milestone achievements (100 platinums, 10,000 trophies)
- Streak achievements (daily logins, consistent syncing)
- Variety achievements (game diversity across platforms)
- Platform mastery achievements
- Completion achievements

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The app is crashing on startup.**

A: Try these steps:
1. Force close the app completely
2. Clear app cache (Android: Settings â†’ Apps â†’ StatusXP â†’ Clear Cache)
3. Restart your device
4. Reinstall the app if issues persist

**Q: My stats look incorrect.**

A: Stats are calculated from your synced data. If something looks wrong:
1. Force a fresh sync on all platforms
2. Check that all your accounts are connected
3. Wait 5-10 minutes for calculations to complete
4. Contact support if issues persist with specific numbers

**Q: I can't sign in with Google/Apple.**

A: Ensure:
- You're using the latest app version
- Your device date/time is correct
- You have a stable internet connection

**Q: Status Posters won't generate.**

A: Status Posters require:
- At least one connected platform with synced data

If it still fails, try force-closing and reopening the app.

**Q: Leaderboards aren't showing my rank.**

A: Leaderboards update every few hours. After your first sync, it may take up to 24 hours to appear. Make sure at least one platform is connected and synced.

### Platform-Specific Issues

**PlayStation (PSN)**

**Q: "Authentication failed" when connecting PSN.**

A: This usually means:
- Incorrect PSN credentials
- Two-factor authentication is enabled (use app-specific password)
- PSN servers are down (check status.playstation.com)

**Q: Hidden trophies aren't showing.**

A: Hidden trophies display once unlocked. For locked hidden trophies, we show the trophy name but keep the description hidden per Sony's policies.

**Xbox**

**Q: "Unable to connect" error with Xbox.**

A: Ensure:
- You're using your Microsoft account email (not gamertag)
- Your Xbox profile is set to "Everyone" in privacy settings
- You're connected to the internet

**Q: Some Xbox achievements are showing as locked when I've earned them.**

A: Xbox achievement sync can have delays. Force a sync and wait 10-15 minutes for the API to update.

**Steam**

**Q: "403 Forbidden" errors during Steam sync.**

A: This means your Steam profile privacy settings are blocking access. To fix:
1. Go to your Steam profile
2. Click "Edit Profile" â†’ "Privacy Settings"
3. Set "Game details" to **Public** (not Friends Only or Private)
4. Also set "My profile" to **Public**
5. Re-run the sync in StatusXP
6. After sync completes, you can change your privacy settings back if desired

**Q: "Invalid Steam ID" error.**

A: You can use either:
- Your numeric Steam ID (found in profile URL: `/profiles/[STEAMID]`)
- Your custom URL (found in profile URL: `/id/[CUSTOMURL]`)

Make sure your Steam profile is set to Public, not Friends Only or Private.

**Q: What do I enter for "Domain Name" when getting a Steam API key?**

A: The "Domain Name" field on steamcommunity.com/dev/apikey is just a label - you can enter anything you want (e.g., "StatusXP", "MyApp", or even "localhost"). It doesn't affect functionality and is only for your reference.

**Q: Steam games missing.**

A: Only games with achievements are shown. Free weekends and deleted games may not appear.
