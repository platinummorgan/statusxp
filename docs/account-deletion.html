StatusXP Account Deletion

We respect your right to delete your account and all associated data. You can request account deletion using either of the methods below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Method 1: In-App Support (Recommended)

  1. Open the StatusXP app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Support
  4. Submit a request with the subject "Account Deletion"

Method 2: Email

Send an email to support@platovalabs.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Please include:

  • The email address associated with your StatusXP account
  • Your username (if you remember it)
  • Any additional information to help us verify your identity

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account credentials (email and password)
  • Your profile information (username, avatar, PSN/Xbox IDs)
  • All your game and achievement data
  • Your flex room data and customizations
  • Your purchase history and premium status
  • All other personal data associated with your account

This action cannot be undone.

Response Time

We will process your account deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data privacy, please contact us at support@platovalabs.com.
