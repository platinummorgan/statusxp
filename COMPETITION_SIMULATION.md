## KEY INSIGHT:

‚úÖ **Locking potential** means:
- You CAN earn points from new games (counts toward numerator)
- Your denominator STAYS THE SAME (doesn't dilute your score)
- Fair competition based on effort, not library expansion

‚ùå **Without locking:**
- Adding games HURTS your score (bigger denominator)
- Punishes players who expand their library
- Creates perverse incentive to NOT buy new games during competition
